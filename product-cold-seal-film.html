Cold Seal Film 冷封膜

Pressure-sensitive sealing film

Cold seal film bonds under pressure without heat, making it ideal for heat-sensitive products such as chocolate, bakery items, and confectionery that would melt under thermal sealing.

Our cold seal coatings provide strong seal strength, clean release, and excellent machinability on high-speed wrapping lines.

Specifications

  • Base films: OPP, PET, paper laminates
  • Seal pattern: full surface or registered strip
  • Food-grade cold seal adhesive
  • Printing: up to 8 colors gravure
  • Applications: chocolate bars, biscuits, cereal bars, chewing gum
