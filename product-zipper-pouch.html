Zipper Pouch 直立拉链袋

Resealable stand-up packaging

Zipper pouches combine shelf presence with convenient reclosability. Consumers can open and reseal the package multiple times, preserving product freshness.

We offer press-to-close zippers, slider zippers, and child-resistant options — all custom printed to your brand specifications.

Specifications

  • Zipper types: standard, slider, pocket zipper
  • Materials: PET/PE, matte BOPP/PE, recyclable PE/PE
  • Bottom styles: round corner, K-seal, plow bottom
  • Printing: up to 10 colors gravure
  • Applications: nuts, dried fruit, supplements, pet treats
