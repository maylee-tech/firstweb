Flat Bottom Bag 平底袋

Premium box-bottom pouch

Flat bottom bags (box pouches) offer five printable panels and a stable base, providing a premium retail appearance similar to rigid boxes at a fraction of the weight and cost.

Ideal for premium coffee, tea, protein powder, and specialty foods that demand strong shelf presence.

Specifications

  • Structure: side gusset + flat bottom with quad seal
  • Materials: PET/AL/PE, kraft paper/PE, matte BOPP/PE
  • Options: zipper, tin tie, degassing valve
  • Printing: up to 10 colors gravure
  • Applications: coffee, tea, nuts, pet food, premium snacks
