Paper Tube

Rigid paper canister packaging

Paper tubes (also known as composite cans or paper canisters) are rigid cylindrical containers made from spiral-wound kraft paper with metal or plastic closures. They offer excellent product protection and premium shelf appeal.

ShenYing produces custom paper tubes for dry foods, supplements, cosmetics, and promotional packaging with full-label printing and various closure options.

Specifications

  • Material: kraft paper + inner liner (PE/Alu/foil)
  • Diameter: 25mm – 150mm (custom sizes available)
  • Height: 50mm – 300mm
  • Printing: full-wrap label or direct printing
  • Closures: metal tin plate, plastic snap lid, resealable lid
  • Applications: protein powder, coffee, tea, snacks, cosmetics, gift packaging
