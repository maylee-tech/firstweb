Sustainable Packaging Trends: Mono-Material Structures in 2026

As global regulations tighten around plastic packaging, brand owners are increasingly adopting mono-material film structures — typically all-PE or all-PP laminates — that can be recycled through existing collection streams.

At ShenYing, we have developed several recyclable pouch structures that maintain barrier performance while meeting emerging sustainability requirements in the EU and North America.

Our Sustainable Solutions

We offer PE/PE stand-up pouches, recyclable zipper films, and downgauged laminates that reduce material usage without compromising package integrity. Our R&D team continues to test compostable and bio-based options for select applications.
