ShenYing Expands Production Capacity with New Pouch Lines

GuangDong ShenYing Packing Co., Ltd is pleased to announce the installation of two new high-speed stand-up pouch making lines at our Dongguan facility. This investment increases our annual production capacity by approximately 30%, enabling us to better serve growing demand from export markets.

The new equipment features advanced servo-driven sealing technology, ensuring consistent seal strength and appearance across high-volume runs. Combined with our existing gravure printing and lamination capabilities, we now offer even faster lead times for large orders.

What This Means for Our Customers

Clients can expect shorter production cycles for stand-up pouches, zipper bags, and flat bottom bags. Our sales and engineering teams remain available to support new projects, sampling, and material optimization.

For inquiries regarding capacity or scheduling, please contact our sales team.
