Team Building Trip to Zhangjiajie Boosts Team Spirit at Shenying Packing

From June 5 to 7, the overseas sales team of Shenying Packing enjoyed a wonderful team building trip to Zhangjiajie, Hunan, famous for its breathtaking natural scenery.

During the three-day getaway, the team hiked through iconic mountain landscapes, joined interactive group games and shared joyful moments together. These fun activities helped everyone release work pressure, enhance communication and build closer bonds between colleagues.

Refreshed and united, our team will carry forward the positive teamwork spirit. We will continue to provide reliable packaging products and thoughtful service for global clients, and make greater progress in international business.
