Roll Film 卷膜

Printed laminated film for FFS machines

Roll film is supplied on reels for automatic form-fill-seal (FFS) packaging lines. We produce custom widths, thicknesses, and laminate structures to match your machine and product requirements.

Each roll is quality-tested for coefficient of friction, seal strength, and print registration before shipment.

Specifications

  • Structures: PET/PE, OPP/CPP, PET/AL/PE, paper/PE
  • Width: 100 – 1200 mm (custom)
  • Core diameter: 76 mm / 152 mm
  • Printing: up to 10 colors gravure
  • Applications: liquid, powder, granule FFS packaging
