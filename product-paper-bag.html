Paper Bag

Eco-friendly paper packaging

Paper bags are a sustainable packaging solution made from kraft paper. They are biodegradable, recyclable, and widely used for retail, grocery, food takeout, and gift packaging.

ShenYing produces custom paper bags with high-quality printing, various sizes, handle options (twisted paper, flat, ribbon), and window cutouts for product visibility.

Specifications

  • Material: kraft paper (virgin/recycled), coated/uncoated
  • Weight: 70–150 gsm
  • Printing: up to 4 colors flexo / offset
  • Options: twisted paper handle, flat handle, ribbon handle, window patch, gusseted sides
  • Applications: retail shopping, grocery, bakery, gift bags, takeout food
