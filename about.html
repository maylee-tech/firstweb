GuangDong ShenYing Packing Co., Ltd

Founded in Guangdong Province, ShenYing Packing has grown into a comprehensive flexible packaging enterprise serving domestic and international markets. We specialize in custom-printed pouches, bags, and laminated films for food, beverage, pet care, and daily consumer products.

Our factory is equipped with gravure printing presses, dry lamination lines, solvent-free lamination, and high-speed bag-making machines. This integrated production chain ensures quality control from raw materials to finished goods.

We believe packaging is more than protection — it is a powerful marketing tool. Our design team helps optimize artwork for maximum shelf impact while meeting functional requirements such as barrier properties, seal strength, and machinability.
