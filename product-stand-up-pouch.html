Stand-Up Pouch 自立袋

Self-standing flexible packaging

Stand-up pouches (Doypack) are one of the most popular flexible packaging formats. The bottom gusset allows the package to stand upright on retail shelves, maximizing visibility and brand exposure.

ShenYing produces custom stand-up pouches with gravure printing, various material laminates, and optional features such as zippers, spouts, and tear notches.

Specifications

  • Materials: PET/PE, PET/AL/PE, kraft paper/PE and more
  • Capacity: 50g – 5kg (custom sizes available)
  • Printing: up to 10 colors gravure
  • Options: zipper, spout, hang hole, matte/gloss finish
  • Applications: snacks, coffee, pet food, sauces, detergents
