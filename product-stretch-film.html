Stretch Film

Pallet wrap for load stability

Stretch film (pallet wrap) is a highly elastic plastic film used to secure products on pallets for storage and transportation. It provides load stability, dust protection, and tamper resistance.

ShenYing supplies high-performance stretch films in hand-wrap and machine-grade options, with custom widths, thicknesses, and colors to meet your packaging line needs.

Specifications

  • Material: LLDPE (linear low-density polyethylene)
  • Thickness: 12–30 microns (custom available)
  • Width: 100mm – 500mm
  • Type: hand wrap / machine wrap / pre-stretched
  • Options: colored film (black, blue, green), UV protection, anti-static, VCI corrosion protection
  • Applications: pallet wrapping, warehouse storage, logistics shipping
