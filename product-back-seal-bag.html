Back Seal Bag 背封袋

Classic pillow pouch packaging

Back seal bags feature a center-back seal creating the familiar pillow shape. They are economical, efficient to produce, and widely used across food and non-food categories.

ShenYing manufactures back seal bags on high-speed lines with precise seal integrity and vivid gravure printing.

Specifications

  • Seal types: center back seal, lap seal
  • Materials: BOPP/CPP, PET/PE, OPP/PE
  • Thickness: 40 – 120 microns
  • Printing: up to 8 colors gravure
  • Applications: candy, grains, powder, disposable items
