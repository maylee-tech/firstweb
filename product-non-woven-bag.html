Non-Woven Fabric Bag

Eco-friendly & reusable shopping bag

Non-woven fabric bags are a popular sustainable alternative to single-use plastic bags. Made from polypropylene (PP) spun-bond fabric, they are lightweight, durable, washable, and fully recyclable.

ShenYing produces custom non-woven bags with high-quality printing, various sizes, colors, and handle options — ideal for retail, grocery, promotional events, and corporate gifts.

Specifications

  • Material: 100% virgin or recycled non-woven polypropylene (PP)
  • Weight: 60–120 gsm (custom weights available)
  • Printing: up to 4 colors gravure / silk screen
  • Options: custom handles (loop, die-cut, long/shoulder straps), inner pouches, zipper closure, gusseted sides
  • Applications: shopping, grocery, promotional giveaways, wine/liquor bags, gift bags
